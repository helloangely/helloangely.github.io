About Angely


Hi! Here's a snipit of my life story:

I was born in New York City, but I lived about 7 years of my early life in India with my grandmother while my mother was studying her graduate degree at Columbia University. In India I attended an International Boarding School where at a young age I was exposed to various cultures around the world, ways of thinking, and communicating. I will be honest - I didn't enjoy being half the world away from my family, but looking back at it I had to grow up quickly at an early age to understand people from many different cultures. My closest friends were from Tibet, Maldives, China, Switzerland, and Germany. To this day I can attribute some of my qualities of easily getting along with anyone to my experiences at International Boarding School.

In the United States, I've lived in New York City, Washington D.C., and Indiana. My favorite of these places is New York City. I love the diversity and entrepreneurial mindset that energizes the city. I attend Indiana University Bloomington, studying Computer Science and Technology Entreprenuership with specializations in Artificial Intelligence and Systems Engineering with minors in Mathematics and Informatics.

I'm obsessed with innovation - I believe that change is exicting, and I want to work with a company that invites fast iterations, new ideas, and people like me eager to make the world more effecient by exploiting the power of code.

In my free time I run, paint with watercolors, eat pizza, read, and practice yoga. I enjoy meeting new people, so if we haven't met please feel welcome to reach out via email.
